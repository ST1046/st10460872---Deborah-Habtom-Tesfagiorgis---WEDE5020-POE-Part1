Exciting News

We are thrilled to announce that Paws has received a ground-breaking donation of R1 million from a generous anonymous benefactor!
This incredible contribution will allow us to expand our facilities, creating additional space for more animals in need.
With this donation, Paws plans to build a new state-of-the-art rehabilitation centre, complete with advanced medical equipment
and dedicated spaces for both small critters and larger animals. This expansion will significantly increase our capacity to rescue and care for stray
and abandoned animals, ensuring they receive the love and attention they deserve. Stay tuned for more updates as we begin
construction on this exciting new chapter for Paws. We are immensely grateful for the support of our community and cannot wait to share the progress with you all!
